Features

Everything you need to
manage attendance

RollDesk packs powerful features into a clean, fast interface. No internet required.

Attendance Tracking

2-step wizard for taking roll call with 5 status options: Present, Absent, Medical Leave, On Duty, and Late.

Elective Management

Create electives, enroll students by RRN, and take separate attendance for elective subjects.

Smart Dashboard

At-a-glance overview with today's timetable, quick stats, recent sessions, and one-tap actions.

Timetable Editor

Weekly class schedule editor with Monday to Friday support, 6 periods per day, and PDF upload.

Reports & Export

Generate attendance reports in PDF, Excel (.xlsx), or CSV format. Share via text or share sheet.

Visual Analytics

Circular and bar charts for per-student and per-subject attendance performance visualization.

Backup & Restore

Export all your data as JSON for safekeeping. Import it back anytime on any device.

Dark Mode

Full light/dark theme support with system default, light, and dark options for comfortable use.

How it works

Three steps to take attendance

RollDesk makes attendance effortless with a streamlined wizard flow.

01

Setup

Import your student roster via CSV, configure your college profile, and set up the weekly timetable.

02

Configure

Select the date, period, and subject from your timetable. RollDesk auto-fills from your schedule.

03

Roll Call

Tap checkboxes to mark attendance. Save, share as text/PDF, or export to Excel in one click.
